## Overview
SYNAPZ Incinerator is a Telegram-based interface for burn tracking and governed burn execution within the SYNAPZ ecosystem.

The bot collects only the minimum data required to operate securely and transparently.

## Data Collected
The bot may process:
- Telegram user ID
- Telegram username (if available)
- Commands sent directly to the bot
- Public blockchain transaction identifiers for burn verification

The bot does NOT collect:
- Private keys or seed phrases
- Personal identity information
- Location data
- Contact lists
- Payment information

## Use of Data
Data is used strictly to:
- Respond to bot commands
- Display burn statistics
- Verify and report on-chain burn transactions
- Enforce admin permissions
- Maintain security and audit logs

No data is used for advertising, profiling, or resale.

## Data Sharing
User data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.
Interactions with public blockchains are inherently transparent.

## Security
The bot does not custody funds or store private keys.
Burn execution is handled by governed backend services.
Sensitive actions are restricted to authorised administrators.

## Data Retention
Minimal operational logs may be retained temporarily and rotated or deleted as required.

## Policy Updates
This policy may be updated as the bot evolves.
Continued use of the bot constitutes acceptance of the current policy.

## Contact
For questions regarding this policy, contact the SYNAPZ team via official SYNAPZ communication channels.
